Background

I grew up tinkering — taking apart computers, building small raspberry pi devices, writing little tools when something annoyed me, and building small games for fun. That turned into a real interest in systems: how software gets built, how data moves, how ML models actually make decisions. I'm now a junior at Bucknell studying Computer Science and Data Science with a 3.66 GPA.

Most of what I build lives at the intersection of backend infrastructure and applied ML: FastAPI services, RAG pipelines, embedding-based retrieval, and deep learning models I train from scratch. I care about shipping things that work end-to-end, not just prototypes.
